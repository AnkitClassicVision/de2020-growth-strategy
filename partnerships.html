Tier 1: High-Priority Partnerships

Critical partnerships for immediate growth impact

Medical Referral Network

Local Primary Care Physicians

Target10-15 PCPs within 20-mile radius
Value for PartnerReliable optometry referrals, diabetic eye care support
Value for DE2020Steady stream of medical eye care patients

Outreach Approach

  • Send introduction letter + capabilities brochure
  • Offer lunch-and-learn on diabetic eye care
  • Establish referral process (fax/electronic)
  • Send quarterly updates on referred patients
Surgical Referral (Two-Way)

Ophthalmology Practices

TargetsVision Care of Maine, Eastern Maine Eye
Value for PartnerReliable optometry partner for routine care
Value for DE2020Surgical referrals, credibility association

Partnership Opportunities

  • LASIK co-management protocol

Corporate Eye Care Program

Local Employers

Partnership Details

TargetEmployers with 25+ employees lacking vision benefits
Value for PartnerEmployee benefit without insurance complexity
Value for DE2020Volume, predictable patient flow

Program Structure

  • Annual eye exams at 15% discount
  • Priority scheduling for employees
  • Optional on-site screening days
  • No insurance paperwork for employer

Target employers: University of Maine departments, small-medium businesses, local manufacturers, professional services firms
