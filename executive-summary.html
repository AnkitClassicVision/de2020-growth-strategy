Business Overview

Concierge optometry with transparent cash pricing

Downeast 20/20 is a concierge optometry practice in Holden, Maine, offering cash-pay eye care services with a specialty focus on dry eye treatment. Founded by Dr. Samuel Valley, a hometown optometrist who returned to serve his rural community, the practice differentiates through personalized care, transparent pricing, and advanced diagnostic technology.
